What is Canyoneering?

Canyoneering is an exhilarating adventure through narrow slot canyons that involves hiking, climbing, rappelling, and swimming through. Utah offers some of the most breathtaking and challenging canyons in the world. I have been canyoneering for years now and love introducing people to the sport!
